Plight of Upcountry Tamils

The history of Upcountry Tamil people in Sri Lanka is marred by a long-standing legacy of discrimination, exploitation, and marginalization. Descendants of laborers brought by the British during colonial rule to work on tea and coffee plantations, estate Tamils have faced systemic challenges that have deprived them of their rights, opportunities, and dignity.
